\section{Conclusion}

\paragraph{Future work.} Several directions extend this work:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{4-player team variant:} Introducing partner signaling (sinais) and cooperative reasoning.
    \item \textbf{Learning agents:} Fine-tuning LLMs on Truco game traces or applying RLHF to improve Truco play.
    \item \textbf{Expanded model coverage:} Evaluating additional open-source models and reasoning-specialized models.
    \item \textbf{Cross-game analysis:} Comparing LLM reasoning patterns across Truco, poker, and other imperfect-information games.
\end{itemize}

TrucoBench is fully open-source. We hope it contributes to a more diverse and culturally inclusive landscape of AI game benchmarks.
